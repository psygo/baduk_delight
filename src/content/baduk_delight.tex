  \chapter{High Level Suicide}
  
  Since everyone is talking about the LG Cup controversy, why not open another can of worms?

  In 2003, during the 15th Kiseong, Cho Hunhyun 9p lost a game to Choi Cheolhan 9p due to an illegal move: suicide. Even Cho himself probably doesn't know what happened. He might have been thinking so far into the future, he conflated the present with it.

  Anyways, even though a pass plus a capture is massive in a professional game, wouldn't you rather have the game continue anyway? (Or maybe at most give it a warning?)

  In my opinion, dropping the suicide rule could enrich Go a tad bit more, creating some more possibilities.
